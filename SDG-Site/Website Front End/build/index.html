The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals


Features

Out of Well offers curriculum for educating students on the seventeen sustainable development goals that were defined by the United Nations (UN). The application creates the perfect learning environment by presenting the content in an interesting manner and forcing the user to use and improve upon their critical thinking skills by presenting solutions to problems concerning the SDG's. A series of short articles and quizzes related to the SDG's are included in the curriculum for supplemental learning outside of the primary game environment. Out of Well strikes the perfect balance between fun and education and integration into traditional school curriculum is simple.
